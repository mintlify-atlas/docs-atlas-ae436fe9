---
title: 'Log in and request an OTP code'
description: 'Step 1 of the AirGuide login flow — submit your email and password to trigger a one-time password sent to your registered email address.'
---

Logging in to AirGuide is a two-step process. In step 1, you submit your credentials to `POST /api/auth/login`. If your email and password are correct and your account is active, the server sends a 6-digit OTP to your registered email. You then proceed to [step 2 — verify the OTP](/auth/two-factor) to receive a JWT token.

## Endpoint

```
POST /api/auth/login
```

## Request body

<ParamField body="correo" type="string" required>
  Your registered email address.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="password" type="string" required>
  Your account password.
</ParamField>

## Response

A `200 OK` response indicating that an OTP has been dispatched:

<ResponseField name="requiresTwoFactor" type="boolean">
  Always `true`. Proceed to `/api/auth/verify-2fa` with the OTP from your email.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="correo" type="string">
  The email address the OTP was sent to. Use this value when calling `/api/auth/verify-2fa`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="message" type="string">
  `"Código de verificación enviado a tu correo electrónico."`
</ResponseField>

## Example

<CodeGroup>

```bash Request
curl -X POST https://your-api.example.com/api/auth/login \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "correo": "estudiante@universidad.edu",
    "password": "MySecurePassword1!"
  }'
```

```json Response
{
  "requiresTwoFactor": true,
  "correo": "estudiante@universidad.edu",
  "message": "Código de verificación enviado a tu correo electrónico."
}
```

</CodeGroup>

<Warning>
  Your account must have `estado: "activo"` to log in. Accounts that are still `"pendiente"` (awaiting admin approval) or `"rechazado"` receive a `403 Forbidden` response with the message `"Tu cuenta aún no ha sido validada. Por favor contacta al administrador."` Contact your administrator to activate your account.
</Warning>

## What to do next

Check your inbox for a 6-digit OTP code and proceed to `POST /api/auth/verify-2fa`. Pass the `correo` value from this response along with the `codigo` from your email to complete login and receive your JWT token.

See [Verify 2FA OTP and receive a JWT token](/auth/two-factor) for the next step.
